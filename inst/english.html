How to use VoteUA service

VoteUA service allows free to add voting, vote, voting their statistics look like.

Add vote can be so: Go to the main page. At the top left you'll see the button "Додати". Click it. Fill in all the lines. Click the "Додати голосування", enter your password. It will need to see their voting statistics. Do not forget your password!

View statistics can be so: Go to the main page. At the top left you will see the "Управління". Click it. Enter your username and password to vote. To exit, click "Log Out".

Voting is very simple. Just go to the official website VoteUA , select voting, click and vote. You can also go straight on at short ballot. To do this, after voteua.github.io/, put a hash (#) and a vote's nick. Each vote is assigned hashvote. This hash symbol (#) and vote's nick. For example, voting with the nickname "vote1", hashvote looks like "#vote1". Putting it on the site, you'll be taken to a voting (for example, voteua.github.io/#vote1). So you can distribute links to their vote.
